Who We Are?

By carrying out the vision of "To be a highly trusted Insurance Company in Indonesia" supported by more than 50 outlets spread throughout Indonesia, at the end of 2015, the Company managed almost 8.8 million units of cover consisting of various products. The products provided consist of conventional and sharia based products. The superior products are motor vehicle insurance products namely car insurance (Autocillin), motorcycle insurance (Motopro), health insurance (Medicillin), and travel insurance (Travellin). The company also provides other products such as Personal Accident Insurance, Property, Heavy Equipment, Shipbuilding, Engineering, Surety Bonds, Transportation, Liability and various other products.

Our vision

To be a highly trusted Insurance Company in Indonesia.

Our Mission

Providing comprehensive insurance products with excellent service. Developing highly competence and professional human resources. Building a strong and competitive institution. Contributing to welfare improvement of Indonesian community.
